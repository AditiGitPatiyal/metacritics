ABOUT US

Welcome to METACRITICS website, the ultimate destination for movie and webseries lovers! Metacritics is a dynamic movie and webseries review website that offers an immersive platform for users to share and explore insightful reviews of the latest films. Metacritics is a comprehensive review website that caters to both critics and users, providing a platform to share and explore thoughtful reviews of the latest movies and web series.

At Metacritics, we believe in the power of diverse perspectives. Our platform brings together professional critics and passionate users, creating a dynamic community that contributes to a rich tapestry of opinions. We understand that everyone has their own unique taste and preferences, so we value both expert critiques and user-generated reviews.

One of the key features of Metacritics is our innovative scoring system. We provide two distinctive scores for each movie or web series: the Metascore and the User Score. The Metascore is an aggregation of ratings and reviews from professional critics. It represents the collective opinion of renowned critics, allowing you to gauge the critical reception of a particular title.

In addition to the Metascore, we also offer a User Score. The User Score is derived from the ratings and reviews submitted by our vibrant community of users. It represents the collective sentiment of the audience, enabling you to assess how well a movie or web series resonates with viewers like yourself.

On Metascore, you can easily navigate through a vast collection of movies and web series, categorized by genre, release date, and more. Each title's page provides comprehensive details, including cast and crew information, plot summaries, trailers, and a curated selection of critic and user reviews.

What's with these green, yellow, and red colors?

It's fairly simple: "good" METASCORES are coded in green; "average" METASCORES are yellow, and "bad" METASCORES are red. (This same color coding is also used for the individual critic and user grades.) If the numbers are too complicated to read, you can simply look at the pretty colors to tell what the reviews said.
